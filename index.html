Welcome

I'm a Full Professor at the Department of Mathematics of the University of Genova, working in Analytic Number Theory.

Home

I work in Analytic Number Theory, in particular on problems related to the Riemann zeta-function, quantum modular forms, and continued fractions, though I have also done research in many other topics. My Settore Scientifico Disciplinare is MATH-03/A - Analisi Matematica.

I am currently the Coordinator for the PhD program in Mathematics for the University of Genova. If you wish to apply, I encourage you to directly contact the professor(s) you would like to work with and to visit our institutional page for more information.
